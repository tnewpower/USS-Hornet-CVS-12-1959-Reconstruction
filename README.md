# USS Hornet (CVS-12) – 1959 WESTPAC Reconstruction

> **An evidence-based historical reconstruction of USS *Hornet* (CVS-12) as she appeared during her 1959 Western Pacific deployment.**

---

## Mission

There is no single USS *Hornet*.

Throughout her service life, *Hornet* underwent continual modification as new equipment was installed, operational requirements evolved, and the U.S. Navy adapted to changing technology. Consequently, every photograph, drawing, and aftermarket component represents the ship at a specific moment in her history.

The mission of this project is to accurately reconstruct **USS *Hornet* (CVS-12) during her 1959 WESTPAC deployment** through careful analysis of primary photographic evidence, historical documentation, and original CAD design.

The completed model, research archive, and supporting documentation are intended to be donated to the **USS *Hornet* Sea, Air & Space Museum** to assist future historians, volunteers, and modelers.

---

## Project Overview

This project combines:

- 📚 Historical research
- 🔍 Configuration analysis
- 🛠️ CAD design
- 🖨️ 3D-printable components
- 🎨 Custom decals
- 📖 Comprehensive documentation

The physical model is based on the **Trumpeter USS Intrepid (CVS-11) 1:350 scale kit**, extensively modified to accurately represent USS *Hornet* during her 1959 Western Pacific deployment.

## Project Goals

- Document the 1959 configuration using primary photographic evidence.
- Produce historically accurate CAD models and STL files.

## Research Philosophy

Every major configuration decision is based on one simple principle:

> **Follow the evidence.**

Whenever possible, conclusions are supported by multiple independent sources, including:

- Cruise books
- Contemporary photographs
- Official drawings
- Museum observations
- Sister ship comparisons
- Physical measurements
- Engineering analysis

Where evidence conflicts, the disagreement is documented rather than ignored.

Where evidence is incomplete, conclusions are presented with an appropriate confidence level.
